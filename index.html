    Features

  • Meaningful Sharing

    ClipMine works great for sharing and embedding recorded video-game sessions, tutorials, products reviews, talkshows, speeches, lectures, and sport coverage. Try out more samples

  • Interactive Embeds

    Want to enable ClipMine experience on your blog or Twitter feed? Just embed our Content-Aware Video Player. It is responsive and build to work across different modern browsers and devices. Start by tagging a video for your choice or pick an already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your website.

  • Smart Tagging

    Found an interesting point in a video? On ClipMine, addind tags is not reserved for the video owners only - any community member can do it. In addition our smart algorithms can automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety of videos!

    • Benefits

  • Personalized Experience

    ClipMine allows your viewers to the video content and see what's inside, through it, and specific parts of the videos they like.

  • Improved Engagement

    Our customers have seen up to 50% higher engagement and lower early drop-off rates on their videos when embedded with ClipMine

  • Actionable Analytics

    Our in-video analytics (by invitations only) allow you to understand user engagement in terms of your content rather than timestamps. You'll exactly know which parts of the video are most likely to be clicked and how long user stay there.
